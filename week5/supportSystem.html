    Student Support System

    We are proud to announce that following student support schemes are being implemented at Govt. Women Engineering College Ajmer under TEQIP-III

    Internship: Students are funded for travel, stay and food expenses incurred during industrial training.

    Induction Program: Induction training program is conducted every year for the new entering students to make them familiar with the college, its functionaries and specially the stream. This reduces their admission pressure and help them to adapt with new place.

    Free GATE coaching: At GWECA our students are given free GATE coaching where they can prepare for higher studies and admission to MTech and get jobs in PSUs.

    Free Placement exams training: Students are trained free of cost for campus placement related written tests of reasoning and general ability which help them to crack written tests of companies.

    Workshops: various technical and professional workshops are organized in the campus for students time to time.

    Interaction with experts industry academia: experts from IITs NITs and multinational companies visit institute campus and interact with students.

    Visit to IITs NITs & research labs: students are financially supported to visit IITs NITs & research labs for study purpose.

    Educational Trips visit to industries: Institute organizes free of cost trips to various industries for the students which helps the students to get the feel of the working culture and procedures of the Industries.

    Support to participate in technical events: students are funded for technical events when they participate in the event. Their travel, food and stay is reimbursed.

    Organize technical event: GWECA organizes state of the art annual techno and cultural event where participants from all over the country are invited and students gets chance to share stage with famous personalities of art, music and academia.

    Our prospective students can also take benefit of these schemes.
