    From the principal desk

    I welcome you to our institute where people feel proud to be a part of it. It is Mahila Engineering College, Ajmer where the Education is imparted to its pupil in such a way to make it an integral part of their life, rather than making it a mere tool for survival of the life.

    View More

    Vision

    To attain excellence in imparting Technical Education to females.

    Mission

    To provide sustainable quality technical education, training and an invigorating environment to young aspirants to promote creativity and stimulate innovative thought process in the students to mould them to meet the growing challenges of the industry and diverse societal needs which will lead to build a powerful and healthy nation.

    1. To impart technical knowledge and infuse a sense of enthusiasm among students to design, create and invent -who possess a knack to design create and develop products and services which will cater to the needs of future generations thereby leading to sustainable development.

    2. To promote women technocrats to make a meaningful contribution by creating a pool of talented human resource - who are capable enough to resolve the problems faced by the country using the knowledge imparted, talent inculcated and the research which we do.

    Student Support Scheme

    The college provides financial and technical support to its students to do internships at renowed industries, provide free training for GATE examination to prepare students for higher studies, preparation for campus interviews. Institude also organizes free visits to various industries, IITs NITs.

    Read More

    Extra-Curricular Activities

    Apart from regular academic schedule students of the college tasks parts in co-corricular activities organized on the campus and at other institutes and have won prizes at different levels. Epistemico is annual cultural fest where all the students, faculty and staff participate with full enthusiasm.

    Read More

    Campus Life

    The college has a lush green campus with facilities such as library, hostel, transport, sports, WiFi, temple, auditorium, seminar hall, conference halls, smart classroom, proposed gymnasium and many more.

    Read More

    Start Up and Incubation Center

    Incubation centre is an initiative of Technical Education Department, Government Of Rajasthan aiming to support and promote innovation, enterpreneurship and create a start-up ecosystem which will inspire the youth to become Enterpreneurs.

    Read More

    Industry Institute Interaction

    One of the primary mission of the college is to prepare its pupil for the industries, so various initiatives are taken such as industries internship, visits to industries, taking inputs from industry professionals to identify areas of training for the students other than regular academics.

    Read More

    Student Body

    Following the Indian system of democracy, a student body is formed by formal election among the students for their representative. The president and vice-president of the student body leads the student activities and represent the student voice for various concerns.
